# User Ordering Guide

This guide explains the public ordering process for customers.

## Placing an Order

1.  **Visit the Campaign Page**: You can find active campaigns listed on the main homepage, or you will be provided a direct link by the fundraiser organizer (e.g., `http://example.com/c/1`).
2.  **Step 1: Select Items**:
    -   Browse the list of available plants/products.
    -   Use the `+` and `-` buttons to adjust quantities.
    -   Click **Continue** when ready.
3.  **Step 2: Your Details**:
    -   Enter your Name, Email, and Phone Number.
    -   This information is used to contact you about pickup and payment.
4.  **Step 3: Review Order**:
    -   Verify your items and contact information.
    -   Click **Submit Order**.
5.  **Confirmation**:
    -   You will see a success message.
    -   **Payment**: Follow the on-screen instructions (e.g., Venmo/Square link) to complete payment.
    -   You will receive a confirmation email (if email service is configured).

## Payment & Pickup

-   **Payment**: Check your confirmation screen or email for payment links. Using the provided Order Number/Name helps identifying your payment.
-   **Pickup**: The organization will notify you of pickup times and locations.
